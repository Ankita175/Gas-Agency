About PetroCore

Petrocore is a leading international oil and gas consulting and logistics services company. Since 1990, we have been a trusted partner for clients in the government and private sectors, providing a comprehensive range of services across the entire oil and gas value chain.

Our Mission

Petrocore is committed to delivering innovative and efficient solutions that empower our clients to achieve their full potential in the oil and gas industry. We are dedicated to:

  • Safety: We prioritize the safety of our employees, partners, and the environment in all our operations.
  • Excellence: We strive for continuous improvement and exceeding client expectations in every project we undertake.
  • Collaboration: We foster strong relationships with our clients and partners, working collaboratively to achieve shared goals.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to responsible development practices and minimizing our environmental impact.

Our Expertise

Petrocore's team of highly skilled and experienced professionals possesses a deep understanding of the oil and gas industry. We are equipped to handle complex projects and deliver results that meet the highest standards.

Our Global Reach

With a presence in USA, UK, France,Singapore and South Africa, Petrocore is well-positioned to serve clients around the world. We leverage our global network and local expertise to provide seamless service delivery wherever your operations are located.
